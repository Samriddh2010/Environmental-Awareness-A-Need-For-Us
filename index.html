INTRODUCTION

Environmental awareness refers to understanding the fragility of our environment an the importance of its protection.
It involves being conscious of how our actions impact the environment and taking steps to reduce our carbon footprint.

WHY IS ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS IMPORTANT??

Environmental awareness is crucial for reducing pollution, conserving resources, promoting health, and fostering sustainable development.

Reasons telling why is awareness important:

  • Reducing pollution helps improve air and water quality
  • Conserving resources ensures they are available for future generations.
  • Promoting health through a cleaner environment.
  • Encouraging sustainable development to maintain ecological balance.
  • Having a better quality of living.
